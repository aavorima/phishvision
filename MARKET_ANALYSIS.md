# PhishVision - Market Analysis & Pricing Strategy 2025

## Executive Summary

The global phishing simulation market is valued at **$1.27-1.5 billion (2025)** and projected to reach **$2.6 billion by 2034** with a CAGR of 7.4-15%. The Middle East cybersecurity market alone is worth **$3.27 billion (2025)** growing to **$5.87 billion by 2030** (12.42% CAGR).

**Key Opportunity:** Only 38% of Middle East employees receive regular cybersecurity training. This awareness gap, combined with a 41% increase in corporate security training engagements, creates a massive opportunity for localized solutions.
